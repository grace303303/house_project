Superfunds

The Superfund program is designed to clean up sites contaminated with hazardous waste. Toxic substances at these sites could poison soil, water or air, causing human health risks like cancer, birth defects, nerve damage and liver damage.

Enviromental Cleanup Sites

Sites require cleanup may impact or have the potential to impact water quality, such as Leaking Underground Storage Tank (LUST) Sites. This map also includes records for various unregulated projects as well as permitted facilities.

Electric Power Transmission Lines

There's a belief that living near power lines may cause illness. Power lines nearby may impact property values because it's an instant turnoff for some home buyers.

Earthquake zones

Earthquake could cause property damage, and people getting hurt or killed. Landslides and liquefaction are secondary effects caused by ground shaking, which are included in this map as well.

Road & Aviation Noise

Noise pollution can be a big factor impacting life quality as well as property values. Home buyers should be on the lookout for excessive noise pollution, including road or highway noise, train noise, and noise from planes flying over your home.

Landfills

Living close to waste solid may result in health risk, which caused by the exposure to air pollutants. High-volume landfills may negatively impact nearby property values while low-volume landfills may not.
